About

The story behind this teaching tool for parents

Why I Built This

When PhonePe changed their UI, my parents thought I had done something to break their phone. I spent 2 hours teaching them the new interface step by step.

My friends told me they face the same problem with their parents too. We all spend valuable time repeatedly explaining the same things.

I built this tool so parents can practice on their own after we teach them personally. It's also helpful for learning existing features they don't know about.

Who Made This
